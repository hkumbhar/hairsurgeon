Who We Are & More

We are Multi-product manufacturing unit of Offset printing, UV printing, Corrugated Boxes, cartons, rolls, brochure catalog leaflet calendar diary pads Packaging materials., serves large base of satisfied corporate clients that include Industry Leaders in the Furniture, Pharmaceuticals, Engineering, Auto Parts, Crockery, Confectionery, Art Materials, Packers & Movers. Over the years Spectrum Printing Solution india has grown to be one of the leading Innovative & Quality supplier of Corrugated Products in the packaging Industry. We are a shipping box company that specializes in custom cardboard boxes. We offer everything you need when it comes to custom sized and printed corrugated shipping boxes.

Spectrum Printing Solution has been designing and manufacturing corrugated boxes for many years. That experience has allowed us to develop the best process for creating perfectly customized shipping boxes and getting them to our customers in an exceptionally timely manner. You can choose from a variety of box styles, from regular slotted container boxes, which are the standard shipping boxes, to more specialized designs, depending on your needs. We can also manufacture your boxes in a range of strengths, so that no matter what you need to pack, move, or ship, our custom containers will hold up.
